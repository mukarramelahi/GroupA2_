Training

TRAINING AREA

Train like a Bear in this elite training area with over 70 pieces of cardio equipment, 5 half racks and platforms, over 5,000 square feet of free-weight training area and tens of thousands of pounds of free weights.

LOCKER ROOMS

Get ready for your workout in our high-end locker rooms. They include sauna, steam rooms and towel service.

FUEL ZONE

Fuel like a Bear. Pick up healthy food options and fresh juices and smoothies at the Fuel Zone, similar to what is offered for players at Halas Hall.

TRAINING TURF

Train on our turf that's 135 feet long and 50 feet wide. We offer functional and group training on the largest indoor fitness turf training space in Chicagoland.

PRO SHOP

Bears fans can pick up exclusive merchandise at the on-site pro shop, which is the team's only pro shop outside of Soldier Field.

KID'S ROOM

Drop your kids off to play games and have fun while you Train Like a Bear.
